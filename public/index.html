About Me

I am a lifelong learner and data enthusiast looking to apply my quantitative research expertise to derive insights from data, with a particular interest in how people interact with and learn from technology. I am currently a postdoc at the University of Colorado, Boulder, where I apply predictive modeling/machine learning techniques to predict reading comprehension from eye movement data, with the goal of improving learning outcomes in real-time. In my free time, I enjoy hiking, playing board games, and reading sci-fi and fantasy novels.
